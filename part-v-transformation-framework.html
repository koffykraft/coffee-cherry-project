Transformation Narrative

Coffee contains potential before flavour exists. That potential is carried in precursor materials and chemical compounds. Roasting transforms those materials through heat, creating new aromas, tastes, and structures. Development guides which aspects become more visible and which become less visible. Transformation continues after roasting through resting, freshness, and aging. The cup is the visible expression of those continuing changes.
