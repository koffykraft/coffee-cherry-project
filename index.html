Coffee Cherry Project

Exploring coffee through observation, tradition, evidence and experience.

This is not primarily a project about coffee cherries. Coffee cherries are the test material. The deeper question is whether the method that emerged during Buna / Citane can travel into another coffee landscape without carrying its old structures with it.
